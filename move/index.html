About us

We are Lukas Flint and Franziskus Hielscher, movers based in Berlin and founders of and coaches at Organic Movement Berlin. Our goal ist to provide you with approved knowledge and technique. To enable the best training experience, we offer both, offline classes in Berlin and other places as well as online training all over the world with our app Organic Movement. Also, on a regular basis we conduct events on different topics and with different teachers to make the world of movement accessible to a wide range of people.
