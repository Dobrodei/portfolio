AI animal health diagnostic system by photo and video analysis

How the system can help

  • Will not miss important details during inspection
  • Highlight problem areas
  • Make a diagnosis and suggest treatment options
  • Keep a visual history of observation/illness of the animal

How HAISCAN work

  • Localizes and highlights anomalies in real time based on the analysis of video and photo images of the animal
  • Analyzes problem areas in detail and gives hints on possible diagnoses based on our own knowledge base
  • Generates a detailed report in a convenient form for further processing and keeps a visual history of observations/illness of the animal
