How It Works: OKToken Growth Mechanism

OKToken maintains its value through:

1

Automated Transaction

When you buy OKToken, a fee is charged. The majority of this fee (70%) goes into a reserve, inaccessible for other purposes, providing an additional level of protection and stability. This is a crucial part of our strategy to maintain the stability and security of your investment.

These funds are used exclusively to maintain the liquidity and stability of OKToken, ensuring your investment retains its value even amid market fluctuations and fostering its growth.

2

Dynamic Supply Management

The OKVault smart contract automatically adjusts the number of tokens in circulation. When demand for the token increases, OKVault releases additional tokens. Conversely, when demand decreases, the contract burns some tokens to reduce their quantity and support the value.

3

Pegging to a Stable Currency

The value of OKToken is automatically adjusted based on its relation to a stable currency (USDT). This sets a fixed exchange rate and minimizes the risks associated with market volatility.
